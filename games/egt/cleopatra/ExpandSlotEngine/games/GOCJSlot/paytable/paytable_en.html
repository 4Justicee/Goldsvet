FREE SPINS

3 or more symbols at any position of the screen trigger 10 FREE SPINS with a SPECIAL EXPANDING SYMBOL. In the beginning of the FREE SPINS one of is randomly selected to act as special expanding symbol. During the FREE SPINS this special symbol expands to cover 3 positions on its reel and pays in any position on lines played. In case of retriggering of the FREE SPINS, the player wins 10 new FREE SPINS with the selected expanding symbol, which are added to the current number of FREE SPINS. The FREE SPINS are played at trigger bet and lines. During the FREE SPINS an alternate set of reels is used.

WILD

is WILD and substitutes for all symbols. A win form a line formed by symbols only is paid as a win from symbols. During the FREE SPINS the WILD is not substituting the selected expanding symbol.

 

JACKPOT CARDS is a four-level mystery jackpot. Each mystery jackpot level is illustrated by a card suit:
(CLUBS) - 1st level (the lowest jackpot value); (DIAMONDS) - 2nd level; (HEARTS) - 3rd level;
(SPADES) - 4th and the highest level.
Any of the jackpot levels can be won during the JACKPOT CARDS Mystery bonus which is randomly triggered after a single game is finished and all resulting winnings are collected.
The player selects cards from the pick field made up of 12 cards face-down until revealing 3 matching card suit symbols. The JACKPOT CARDS Mystery level corresponding to the revealed suit will be awarded. The bonus amount will correspond to the amount accumulated at the moment of revealing the last matching symbol, shown on the right side of each card suit.
The player has a reasonable chance to win the highest level of the jackpot with any of the qualifying bets, every time when he/she enters the JACKPOT CARDS bonus.
Qualifying bets: 100 - 500

GAMBLE

To gamble any win less than 2000

press GAMBLE button, then select RED or BLACK. If RED/BLACK choice is correct the gamble amount is doubled (x2). If RED/BLACK choice is not correct the gamble amount is lost and the game is finished.

PAYLINES AND RULES

 

All pays are left to right on adjacent reels, on selected lines, beginning with the leftmost reel, except scatters. Scatter wins are added to the payline wins. Highest payline and/or scatter wins only paid. Line wins are multiplied by the bet value on the winning line. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total bet value.
During the free spins the special expanding symbol pays in any position on lines played.
Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
